Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Atomic Kids

What age group is Atomic Kids designed for?
Atomic Kids is designed for children ages 4-12, with customizable features that adapt to different developmental stages and abilities.
Can parents customize the habits and rewards?
Absolutely! Parents have full control to create custom habits based on their family's needs and values, as well as personalized rewards that motivate their specific child.
Is there a subscription fee?
Atomic Kids offers both a free version with basic features and a premium subscription that unlocks additional habit templates, advanced analytics, and exclusive reward content.
How does the app protect my child's privacy?
We take privacy seriously. Atomic Kids is COPPA-compliant, doesn't collect unnecessary personal information, and all data is encrypted. Parents have complete control over account settings.
